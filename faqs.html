Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is where you turn your crazy ideas into real projects. We provide hardware, mentors, fun activities, speakers, workshops, food, friends, and so much more. You take care of hacking; we’ll take care of you.

How do I get there?

The event will be held in Brampton's City Hall. Location Address: 2 Wellington St W, Brampton, ON L6Y 4R2

What if I don't have a team?

We will host a team formation event at the beginning of the hackathon so there's no need to hack alone (unless you want to go solo!)

What can I build?

Anything your heart desires! At TurnerHacks you'll have access to all the hardware technologies you need, ranging from VR to gaming, we have partnered with several start-ups and companies to make sure we got 'em all!

What if I have never coded?

You don't need to know how to code! We have range of workshops during the hackathon that will introduce you to programming languages and developing for hardware!

Will you provide hardware?

Yes! There’ll be tons of cool hardware for you to hack on! We’ll try to put out a list closer to the event so you can see what’s available. Feel free also to bring your own from home or email us with any suggestions!

How is TurnerHacks different?

TurnerHacks is a hackathon for students in the Peel region. Have no fear of university sweats stealing your first place prize!

Who can attend?

You are eligible to attend if you're currently attending a peel district school.

Have some other questions?

You can email us to any questions you still have.
