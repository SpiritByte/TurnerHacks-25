Peel District School Board

The Peel District School Board (PDSB) serves over 148,000 students across Brampton, Mississauga, and Caledon — making it one of the largest and most diverse school boards in Canada. With students from 188 countries speaking 121 languages, PDSB is all about inclusion, innovation, and preparing learners for a global future. From French immersion to adult education and equity-focused programs, the board is committed to helping every student succeed in a safe, respectful, and empowering environment.

Algoma University

Algoma University is a small but mighty school with campuses in Sault Ste. Marie, Brampton, and Timmins. Known for its supportive community, small class sizes, and hands-on learning, it offers programs in arts, sciences, business, and more. With a strong focus on cross-cultural understanding — especially through its deep connection to Indigenous history — Algoma blends academic excellence with a mission that matters. It’s a place where students feel seen, supported, and ready to succeed.

Zebra Technologies

Zebra Technologies is leading the way in data-driven solutions that improve efficiency and visibility across industries. Their advanced mobile computers, barcode scanners, RFID tech, and real-time data platforms help businesses make quicker, smarter decisions. Focused on automation and AI, Zebra is innovating with intelligent edge devices and machine vision systems that streamline workflows and reduce errors. From retail to healthcare and logistics, Zebra is empowering businesses to optimize operations in a connected world.
